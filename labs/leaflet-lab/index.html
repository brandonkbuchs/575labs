National Park Visitation Statistics Map | Brandon Buchs

This interactive map allows users to look at NPS reported visitation statistics by year for National Parks. It also allows the user to adjust the symbol scale.

The data available is from 2000-2018. The scale factor adjust from 1 to 5.

To adjust the year, click on the slider arrows or move the slider. To adjust the symbol scale, use the symbol scale slider. Click on any symbol for a detailed display of information.

NPS data provided from IRMA on NPS | Map Tileset created by Brandon Buchs on mapbox
